# DQAT-5 - Future Date of Birth Allowed

## Description
System allows selection and submission of future dates in Date of Birth field.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open Practice Form page
2. Open Date Picker
3. Select a future date (e.g., 01 Jan 2030)
4. Submit form

## Expected Result
System should prevent selection or submission of future dates

## Actual Result
Future date is accepted and form is submitted successfully

## Severity
High

## Priority
High

## Environment
- Browser: Chrome
- URL: https://demoqa.com
